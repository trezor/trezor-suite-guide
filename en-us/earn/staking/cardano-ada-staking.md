# Cardano (ADA) staking

Stake your ADA in Trezor Suite by delegating your full account balance to a Trezor staking pool. Your ADA stays liquid while staked. You can send it at any time. Rewards are distributed each epoch (approximately 5 days) and compound automatically. Note that rewards don't start until the fourth epoch after you delegate.

A refundable deposit of 2 ADA is required to register your stake key. You get it back when you unstake.

Cardano staking is available on Trezor Model T, Safe 3, Safe 5, and Safe 7. It is not supported on Model One.

1. Select your Cardano account in the left sidebar.
2. Open the **Staking** tab and click **Start staking**.
3. Confirm the stake key registration on your Trezor device.
4. Accept the terms and click **Continue**.
5. Confirm the delegation on your Trezor device.

You can unstake or change your pool at any time from the Staking tab.
