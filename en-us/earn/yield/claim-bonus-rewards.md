# Claim bonus rewards

On top of regular vault yield, a vault may run a temporary bonus campaign that pays extra rewards in MORPHO tokens. These come from Morpho's incentive programs and are distributed through Merkl, a rewards platform.

When you have MORPHO to claim, a **Claim** button appears in the Earn tab. One signed transaction on your Trezor collects your MORPHO along with any other pending rewards. Anything you've earned stays claimable even after a campaign ends.

{% hint style="info" %}
You can check whether a campaign is currently running at [app.merkl.xyz/?search=Trezor](https://app.merkl.xyz/?search=Trezor).
{% endhint %}
