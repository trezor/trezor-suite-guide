# Withdraw stablecoins

You can withdraw at any time. Only the Ethereum network fee applies.

1. Click **Earn** in the sidebar and scroll to **Stablecoin yield**.
2. Find your active vault and click **Withdraw**.
3. Enter the amount to withdraw, or click **Max** for the full amount.
4. Click **Withdraw**.
5. Review the transaction simulation and click **Confirm**.
6. Confirm on your Trezor device and click **Withdraw**.

Your stablecoins (plus any yield earned) arrive in your account once the transaction settles on-chain.
