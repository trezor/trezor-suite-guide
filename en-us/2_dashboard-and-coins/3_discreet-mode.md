# Hide balances

The **hide balances** feature blurs account balances and any other sensitive data displayed in Trezor Suite, only revealing it on mouse-over.

To activate the feature, click the eye icon in the bottom-left corner of Trezor Suite:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Hide_Dark-HL.webp" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click the button again to reveal the hidden information.
