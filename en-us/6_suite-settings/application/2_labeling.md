# Labeling

Labeling is a tool that helps you stay organized. Rename wallets, accounts, and addresses to help keep track of your portfolio.

### Suite Sync

> ℹ️ Suite Sync is compatible with all **Trezor Safe** family devices.

Keep your labels private, synced across devices, and recoverable without relying on third-party services. Suite Sync **stores your data locally** on your device with encrypted backups, so **only you can read it**.

- Go to **Settings > Application** and scroll down to the **Labeling** section
- Select **Suite Sync** from the drop-down menu
- To start using Suite Sync, you only have to confirm it once on your Trezor Safe hardware wallet.

Your labels are synced via the Trezor servers by default. If you want to connect to a custom server:

- Select **Change server**
- In the **Suite Sync server** window, select **Custom server** from the drop-down menu
- Enter your server address in the input field and click **Confirm** to finish the setup

### Legacy Labeling

> ℹ️ Legacy Labeling is compatible with **all** Trezor hardware wallets.

Labels are synced to your Google Drive or Dropbox account, or they can be stored locally. All the information you add is automatically encrypted by a key derived from your Trezor device.

- Go to **Settings > Application** and scroll down to the **Labeling** section
- Select **Legacy** from the drop-down menu
- Press **Enable** on your Trezor
- In the **Save labels** window, select how to sync your labels

> 💡 Learn more about [Suite Sync](https://trezor.io/guides/trezor-suite/suite-sync) and [Labels in Trezor Suite](https://trezor.io/guides/trezor-suite/labels-in-trezor-suite) on the Trezor knowledge base
